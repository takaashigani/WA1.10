Conway's Game of Life

10
  1. Any live cell with fewer than two live neighbours has a chance of 100% to die, as if caused by underpopulation.
  2. Any live cell with two or three live neighbours has a chance of 100% to live on to the next generation.
  3. Any live cell with more than three live neighbours has a chance of 100% to die, as if by overpopulation.
  4. Any dead cell with exactly three live neighbours has a chance of 100% to become a live cell, as if by reproduction.
